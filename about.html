About us

Universal College under the aegis of Universal Educational Trust (R) made a humble beginning in 1970 in its journey of higher education with a view to provide quality education to build and shape the individuals who are relevant to the competitive world. The institution Është involved in various innovative programmes apart from teaching regular curriculum. These programmes are carefully designed to provide students a clear edge in placement or further education. The college has excelled in all disciplines, curricular, extracurricular and co-curricular activities. The Quality initiatives undertaken by the college, has resulted in an increased demand for the seats. Developing Universal College as a campus of excellence is a big challenge to the management and the authorities are well prepared and equipped in planning and promoting need based strategies.

Vision

To help students acquire and develop knowledge, skills, values & leadership qualities relevant to the 21st century and beyond for their personal and professional growth.

Mission

To make Universal College a nurturing ground for the holistic development of young minds by providing intellectual nourishment and wholesome education that sustains emotional stability and strength to respond creatively to the challenges of a world full of relentless competition.

Objectives

The main objective is to expand student’s intellectual and spiritual horizons, underscore the nobility of humanity’s never-ending search for truth, beauty and compassion, by focusing student-centered teaching-learning activities.

Core Values

We have articulated six core values that guide our operational ethos and commitment to deliver unparalled support for our students.

  • Accountability
  • Collaboration
  • Empowerment
  • Integrity
  • Development
  • Enrichment
  • Exploration
