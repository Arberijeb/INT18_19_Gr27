Department of Power Engineering

The Department Power Engineering was established from the inception of the institute in the year 2008. The department offers one under graduate B.Tech. (P.E) programme and one Post Graduate (M.Tech) programme in "Power Electronics" specialization. The department has been continuously striving for excellence in engineering education by well designed, constantly reviewed program to incorporate all advancements in existing and emerging technologies which gives the students a holistic and sensible view of the present scenario of the Electrical industry.

The under graduate program imparts students with professional in the field of power engineering besides expert in mathematics, physics, computers, measurements and instrumentation are the essential need for any flourishing electrical engineer. Students gain practical experience from field visits to electrical machine manufacturing industries, power generating stations, substations and power grid corporations.
