INVITATION

Support Kosova’s Next Success Story

The Board of Trustees, President and UCOE (Universal College Of Engineering) community, invite you, your family and your friends to join us for a special event in New York metropolitan area to Support the Kosova’s Next Success Story


We are proud to witness how our institution has positively impacted Kosovar society through alumni engagement. Our current Success Stories are best represented by our Alumni:

  • 68% of alumni hold management positions; whereas 53% are mid and senior managers.
  • 11% of our Alumni are entrepreneurs and employ more than 1,000 Kosovars.
  • 95% of our Alumni are working.
  • Of alumni who pursued master’s degrees, 65% received some form of scholarship, and 47% were awarded full scholarships to study abroad.

While we have great cases of success to share with you, we must acknowledge that students from Kosova need further support to Become the Next Success Stories. Supporting quality education and building the future leaders of Kosovo is one of the best ways to consolidate the statehood of Kosovo and the advancement of this society. 

Your attendance at this cocktail supports and encourages our talented and underprivileged students to aspire for the best education possible in Kosovo and the region.

The Support Kosova’s Next Success Story event will enable our university to offer more scholarships for talented students with limited financial means from Kosovo and abroad. This event is a great opportunity for our Albanian diaspora and other friends in United States to learn about the Universal College of Engineering in Kosovo, its impact in the country and how your contribution can make an enormous difference.

When:
Wednesday, January 30, 2019 | 6:30 – 9:00 p.m.
Where:
Harvard Club, New York
35 West 44th Street
New York, NY 10036

We want to continue doing our best, and we need your involvement to Support Kosova’s Next Success Story and help them to attain an excellent American education! Please come and join us for some food and drinks and be ready to be amazed by the incredible and inspiring stories we will share with you.

Sincerely,

Board of Trustees | Universal College of Engineering
